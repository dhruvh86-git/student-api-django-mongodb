Q: Why MongoDB?
A: "Flexible schema, JSON-native, good for document storage. 
    For this simple CRUD, it's perfect. In production with 
    complex relationships, I'd consider PostgreSQL."

Q: Why not Djongo?
A: "Dependency conflicts with Django 4.2. PyMongo gives direct 
    control, better performance, and is production-ready."

Q: Authentication?
A: "Would add JWT tokens with djangorestframework-simplejwt, 
    role-based permissions, and rate limiting."

Q: Testing?
A: "Unit tests for serializers, integration tests for API 
    endpoints, load testing with Locust/JMeter."

Q: Deployment?
A: "Railway/Render for Django, MongoDB Atlas for database, 
    with environment variables for config. Add CORS, logging, 
    monitoring, and CI/CD."

Q: Scalability?
A: "Add caching (Redis), database indexing, pagination, 
    horizontal scaling with load balancer."

Q: Data integrity?
A: "Three layers: MongoDB unique indexes, serializer validation, 
    view-level duplicate checks. Would add soft deletes and 
    audit logs in production."
